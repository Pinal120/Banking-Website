Our History

Founded in 1995, EastBank started with a simple vision: to provide accessible, secure, and reliable banking services to individuals and businesses alike.

In the years that followed, EastBank expanded to multiple locations across the United States, and later, internationally.

Our Mission

At EastBank, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses by providing innovative financial solutions that meet their needs and goals.

We are dedicated to fostering financial literacy and promoting responsible banking practices.

Our Core Values

At EastBank, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and responsibility. Our core values guide everything we do:

  • Customer-Centricity: We put our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and innovation to provide efficient, modern banking solutions.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business.
  • Community Focus: We are committed to giving back to the communities we serve.

Our Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, EastBank plans to continue its expansion both locally and globally, with a focus on enhancing the digital banking experience.

Our vision is to build a global network of customers who are empowered to manage their finances effortlessly and effectively.

Our Commitment to Diversity & Inclusion

At EastBank, we are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment, both for our employees and customers.

We actively support diversity through our hiring practices, community outreach programs, and internal initiatives.
